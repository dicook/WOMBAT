How to Get the Most out of Your Time

Amy Cook, Bayesian Research and Applications Group, QUT

Timesheet software for consulting companies stores complex project information for its managers, but has not been designed with data analysis in mind. The software collects troves of information that would be suitable for analysis: financial performance of projects, client information, hours and timespans. After working in a consulting company for a few years, I decided to extract as much historic project data as possible, and see what I could discover in a friendlier environment: R.
