Making Data Analysis Easier

Workshop Organised by the Monash Business Analytics Team (WOMBAT)

Feb 18-19, 2016 at the Melbourne Zoo

Keynote
Dr Hadley Wickham
Chief Scientist, RStudio
Topic: TBD

Dr Wickham's work is focused on making data analysis easier for the community. He has been instrumental in changing the way we think about data analysis, and providing new tools for tidying, rearranging, summarising and plotting data. His R packages, tidyr, dplyr, ggplot2, ggvis, are open source and widely used.
